Purpose of this site

The purpose of this website is to present the different types of cloud.
The natural phenomena of our planet can sometimes be considered as boring for the majority of people, but well presented they can appeal and make people reconsider all the other "boring" subject as maybe not so boring.

3 types of cloud

There is 3 types of cloud and they are classified based on the height of the cloud from the surface of the earth.
It gives us:

  • Low clouds, the closer from the surface of the earth.
  • Middle clouds, higher than the lowers ones.
  • High clouds, even higher than the middle ones.
